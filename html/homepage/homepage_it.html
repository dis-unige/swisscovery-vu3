Welcome to swisscovery UNIGE

Here you can find books, ebooks, articles, journals, databases and other documents from the collection of the University of Geneva Library (swisscovery UNIGE) but also from the other scientific libraries of the City of Geneva (swisscovery Geneva) and the 490 swiss academic libraries (swisscovery and swisscovery plus)

Search Scopes

swisscovery UNIGE

  • Physical and digital collections of the University of Geneva Library and the Hardt Foundation for the Study of Classical Antiquity
  • References of the UNIGE Open Archive


   Document orders are free but only possible for DBU documents (to all campus locations) and Uni Bastions Battelle documents (to Uni Bastions Jura)

swisscovery Geneva

Collections of Geneva institutions:

  • University of Geneva Library, Fondation Hardt and UNIGE Open Archive
  • Bibliothèques scientifiques de la Ville de Genève : BGE, BAA, CJBG, FMAC, IMV, MEG, MHS, MHN, Musée Ariana, Archives de la Ville et Bibliothèque musicale
  • Bibliothèque de l'Institut de Hautes Etudes Internationales et du Développement (IHEID)
  • Bibliothèques genevoises de la Haute Ecole Spécialisée de Suisse Occidentale (HES-SO) : HEAD, HEG, HEdS et HETS
  • Autres bibliothèques spécialisées : Archives d'Etat, Pouvoir judiciaire, Conseil oecuménique des Eglises (COE) et Bibliothèque Filigrane

swisscovery

  • Collections of all libraries in the SLSP network


   You can order documents via the shuttle service (see details)

swisscovery plus

  • Collections of all libraries in the SLSP network
  • Resources of the Renouvaud network (libraries of the Canton of Vaud)
  • Digital resources without full text


   Documents from the Renouvaud network can be ordered via the shuttle service (see details)

How much does it cost?

Access to the rooms and collections of the University of Geneva Library is free and open to all, including the registration and borrowing of documents. However, there may be a charge for late returns or for ordering documents (see details).
